The Original Lucky Bill | Garapan, Saipan

I chose The Original Lucky Bill as my top 1 because it has variety of American and Thai cusine, what I liked the most was their side dishes which was somtam, papaya salad and you can adjust the spice level (thai are known for their spice), their fried rice and other authentic thai dishes. The people were very friendly and welcoming and the setting was cozy that it felt like home.

    What I love:
  • Friendly Service
  • Delicious SomTam (papaya salad)
  • Cozy Atmosphere
